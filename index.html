What Gemini is great at — and where Super goes further

Gemini

Gemini is Google’s flagship AI assistant, spanning chat, search, mobile, and now desktop automation. With the launch of Gemini Spark on macOS, Gemini can organize files, automate multi‑step tasks, connect to Google Workspace, and integrate with third‑party apps like Canva and Dropbox.

  • Deep Google ecosystem integration
  • Strong conversational and planning abilities
  • Agentic features via Spark on Mac

Super

Super is focused narrowly on durable computer‑use workflows. Its defining advantage is a reusable computer-use cache, so repeated browser and desktop actions don’t cost the same every time.

  • Agents that operate real computers
  • Cache reuse for repeated workflows
  • Better fit for ongoing operational work

Why Gemini Spark matters — and its limits

Desktop automation arrives

In mid‑2026, Google rolled out Gemini Spark to macOS, enabling local file access, desktop automation, and multi‑step tasks executed directly on a user’s Mac.

Connected apps & MCP

Spark connects to Google Tasks, Keep, and third‑party services, and adds support for custom Model Context Protocol (MCP) integrations, expanding what Gemini agents can touch.

General agent, general costs

Gemini Spark excels at broad, one‑off automation. But repeated computer‑use workflows still re‑execute from scratch, without a persistent execution cache optimized for reuse.
